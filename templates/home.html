Welcome to CamLive

The video below explains how to use the website to link your cameras.

Our website uses AI technology to count the people in your organization and notify you if the count exceeds the allowed limit.

Other features include:

  • Showing a graph of the number of visitors throughout the day.
  • Providing visitor data for each day from the day you link your cam.
  • Allowing you to upload a video from your desk and start counting visitors.

The best thing is that you can monitor your organization, organize crowds, and prevent traffic all at the same time.
